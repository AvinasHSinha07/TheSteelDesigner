The Workflow

A transparent, linear path from architectural concept to structural reality.

PHASE 01

Brief & Scope Definition

Analysis of architectural intent. We define the Eurocode parameters, loading conditions, and deliverables list.

PHASE 02

Analytical Modelling

Building the computational model in SAP2000/ETABS. Iterative sizing of members for optimal weight-to-performance ratio.

PHASE 03

Detailing & Handoff

Synchronization with Tekla Structures. Production of General Arrangement drawings, Assembly lists, and CNC data.
